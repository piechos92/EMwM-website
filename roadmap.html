System Roadmap
 

1. Knowledge Board

Release: April 6, 2025 ⓘ

🛠️ Problems It Solves:

  • Knowledge is scattered across apps, websites, and devices.
  • Important information gets lost in the noise.
  • No unified system to organize and retrieve your learning.
  • Hard to share knowledge with your team or community.

✅ What User Gains:

  • One central place for all your knowledge assets.
  • Visual organization of ideas, notes, and resources.
  • Quick access to what you need, when you need it.
  • Effortless knowledge sharing with collaborators.

Description: Central dashboard that organizes all your learning resources, reading lists, and knowledge assets in one visual interface for easy access and management.

2. Knowledge Consumption Agent

Release: April 6, 2025 ⓘ

🛠️ Problems It Solves:

  • Reading and processing content takes too much time.
  • Important insights get lost when consuming content.
  • Manual note-taking breaks your reading flow.
  • Difficult to connect new knowledge with existing concepts.
  • No system to track what you've learned and apply it.

✅ What User Gains:

  • AI-powered capture of key insights while you read.
  • Automatic organization of new knowledge into your system.
  • Seamless connection of new ideas with existing knowledge.
  • Save hours of manual processing and note-taking.
  • Track and apply what you're learning more effectively.

Description: AI-powered assistant that helps you capture, process, and integrate new knowledge as you read articles, books, or take courses, saving hours of manual work.

3. Contact Board with Agents

Release: May 25, 2025 ⓘ

🛠️ Problems It Solves:

  • Contacts are scattered across tools and hard to manage.
  • You forget who people are or where you met them.
  • No single place to track relationships, attributes, or context.
  • You don't remember who you know in key industries or with relevant skills.
  • Important follow-ups are missed or forgotten.
  • Entering and updating contact data manually is time-consuming and often skipped.
  • The system becomes outdated if not reviewed regularly.
  • You lack the time or discipline to maintain your network consistently.

✅ What User Gains:

  • All your contacts, attributes, and interactions in one smart, searchable place.
  • Voice-based agent lets you add, edit, and search contacts without typing.
  • See relationship maps: who knows whom, who works with whom.
  • Smart detection of neglected contacts and upcoming follow-ups.
  • Weekly AI-guided review that keeps everything up to date — even if you're busy.
  • Peace of mind that your contact network is alive, organized, and ready when you need it.

Description: Comprehensive contact management system with AI agents that help you maintain, organize, and leverage your professional network effectively.
